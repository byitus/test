World-Class Facilities

OT
Theatre Facility
4 theatres (3 major + 1 minor), laparoscopic, endoscopy, C-Arm, operating microscopes & defibrillators.
24H
24-Hour Pharmacy
Round-the-clock pharmacy ensuring medicines are available whenever patients need them.
ICU
ICU Ward
7 beds with invasive & non-invasive monitors, pulse oximeters, infusion pumps, central oxygen & suction.
CCU
CCU Ward
7 beds with 2 ventilators, ECG, defibrillator, central oxygen and full monitoring capabilities.

NICU Ward
4 beds with incubator, warmer, photo therapy unit, infusion pump and ventilator for neonatal care.
